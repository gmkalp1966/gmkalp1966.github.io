SPORT AND EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

We believe that every child is capable, be it academics, sports or extra curricular activities. Atomic Energy Central School No.2 offer all students more than one avenue to develop, discover and nurture his/her talents. Every student is encouraged to participate in debates, elocution, quiz competitions, dance, drama, music and outdoor activities like hiking, trekking and boating (Kodai) and to enjoy their participation in such events. The schools encourage the physical development of all the students.

AECS-2, Kalpakkam believe that every child should be given the opportunity to excel in academics and sports. Every school offers a wide variety of sporting activities like-cricket, football, hockey, basket-ball, badminton, table tennis and gymnastics. Besides the needed physical exercise, they impart valuable lessons in leadership, discipline, competitiveness, fair-play, perseverance and team spirit.

Co-curricular & Extra - Curricular activities:

AECS-2 , Kaplapakkam offer a wide variety of extra-curricular activities that help students imbibe valuable lessons in social development and responsibilities. The schools offer every student a course in NSS and NCC. Diverse activities like music, dance, theatre, creative writing, debates and nature clubs encourage student development.
