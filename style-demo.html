MISSION STATEMENT

Atomic Energy Education Society(AEES) endeavours to empower its students with wide spectrum of knowledge to prepare them to become responsible citizens of the country. Furthermore, AEES is determined to inculcate in its students attitude and expertise required for the worldliness, patriotism, exemplary moral values and respect towards the rich culture of the Nation. We also encourage our students for eradication of differences based on caste, creed, gender, religion and language.

Atomic Energy Central School -2, Kalpakkam aids the development of the minds of its students in a manner that helps them grow into personalities of distinction. The infrastructure, in-depth curriculum and skilled faculty help each and every child realise his/her true potential and go one step towards perfection.

Atomic Energy Central School -2, Kalpakkam– “ A Stepping stone to a brighter future.”

THE THREE BASIC AIMS THAT GOVERN OUR EFFORTS ARE:-

Intellectual Development : Empowering every student with the skills and knowledge required to cope with the competitive challenges of the complex world.

Character Development : The schools instill in every pupil the basic human values and pride by drawing examples from our rich and varied heritage.

Physical Development : A right mixture of sports & extra curricular activities ensures an overall physical development in every student.


Atomic Energy Central School -2, Kalpakkam, education is not merely imparting facts but leading every young scholar in his/her chosen path individually. The schools have scripted success for the students by creating an environment conducive for emotional, physical and intellectual growth. The Schools encourage every student to set up his/her own goals and guide them to pursue their goals with the teacher's guidance.
