FROM PRINCIPAL'S DESK

Education is the reconstruction or reorganization of experience which adds to the meaning of experience, and which increase the ability to direct the couse subsequent experiences.

- John Dewy

The root meaning of Education is bringing out or making manifest the inherent potentialities in a pupil. Broadly speaking, it is a process of deliberate transmission of our cultural heritage, moral values, knowledge and skill from one generation to another. Keeping this in mind, our school aims at preparing the students under our care as the propagators of knowledge, skills, values, rich cultural tradition, heritage etc.

Our school, with its dedicated team of teachers, sincerely strives to inculcate the aforesaid values to the students through co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, besides the curricular pursuits.

This website not only throws light on the pupils' intellectual vigour and vitality but also their multifaceted personality in the fields of academics, sports and games, external competitive exams, state / national wize quiz competitions, special programmes, NCC, NSS, Scouts and Guides and What not...! It is our attempt to present a bird;s eye-view of our school's multifarious useful and productive activities.
