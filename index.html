  • OUR SCHOOL

    AECS-2, Kalpakkam is 80 km from Chennai situated on the picturesque shores of Bay of Bengal.  It really has a peaceful ambience suitable for teching and learning.  AECS-2, Kalpakkam, was bifurcated into AECS-1 and AECS-2, during 2001-02, the school had classes from II to XII. Class I started in the year 2003.
